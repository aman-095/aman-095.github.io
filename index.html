About Me

Hi, I am Aman Bhansali, pursuing my B-Tech in the Mechanical Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. Applications like machine learning, data science, and natural language processing that address challenges in the real world greatly fascinate me. I explored the field and created my projects in this area after discovering my interests during the coursework session. I work diligently and honestly, and I am passionate and enthusiastic about this industry.

I have worked on a Research Project at IIT Patna under the supervision of Prof. Sriparna Saha called "Towards Query Semantic Knowledge Guided Multi-Span Medical Question Answering" which intends to enhance the medical context summarizer with multi-spanned answers provided a question. Since a lot of text is available today for a single question, it becomes a challenge to identify the most pertinent information. Our results have surpassed the existing state-of-the-art model with around a 2.5-4% increment in terms of F1 score and exact match.
